Our History

Hope Harvest Community Garden began as a small neighborhood project in 2015. Over time, it grew into a citywide initiative addressing food insecurity through fresh produce and gardening education.

Our Core Values

  • Community: Building strong bonds among residents through shared work.

  • Sustainability: Promoting environmentally friendly gardening methods.

  • Education: Empowering residents with knowledge to grow their own food.

  • Equity: Ensuring healthy food access for everyone, especially low-income families.
