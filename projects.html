Current Projects

  • Urban Beds Expansion
    Expanding raised garden beds to provide more fresh produce for local food parcel programs.
  • School Garden Mentors
    Pairing volunteers with schools and youth groups to teach children about sustainable gardening.
  • Community Compost Hub
    Establishing a shared composting site to reduce waste and provide nutrient-rich soil for our gardens.

Past Projects

  • Rainwater Harvesting System
    Installed in 2021 to ensure a sustainable water supply for our crops during dry months.
  • Pollinator-Friendly Garden
    Completed in 2020 to support bees and butterflies, boosting both biodiversity and crop yields.
  • Seed Sharing Initiative
    Launched in 2019 to help families start their own gardens with free starter seeds.
